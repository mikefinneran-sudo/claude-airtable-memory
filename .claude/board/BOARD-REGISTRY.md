## Meeting Structure

### Quarterly Board Meetings (4 hours)
- Strategic review and guidance
- Financial oversight
- Major decision advisory
- CEO performance feedback

### Annual Strategic Planning (Full day)
- 3-year strategic plan
- Budget and resource allocation
- Major initiatives

### Rotating Thought Leader Sessions (Quarterly, 90 minutes)
- Fresh perspectives from unrelated industries
- Creativity and innovation sparks
- Challenge assumptions
