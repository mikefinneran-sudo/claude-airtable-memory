## Meeting Structure

### Quarterly Board Meetings (4 hours)

**Agenda**:
1. CEO Report (30 min)
2. Financial Review (45 min)
3. Strategic Deep Dive (90 min)
4. Board Advisor Input (45 min)
5. Executive Session (30 min)

**Preparation**:
- CEO sends board packet 1 week in advance
- 50-100 pages: financials, metrics, strategic topics
- Board members read and prepare questions

**Output**:
- Meeting minutes
- Action items for CEO
- Strategic guidance
- Performance feedback

### Annual Strategic Planning (Full day)

**Focus**:
- 3-year strategic plan
- Annual OKRs and goals
- Budget and resource allocation
- Major initiatives

### Quarterly Thought Leader Sessions (90 min)

**Format**:
1. Thought leader presentation (30 min)
2. Facilitated discussion (45 min)
3. Synthesis and takeaways (15 min)

**Goal**: Fresh perspectives, challenge assumptions, spark creativity

## Key Decisions Requiring Board Input

### Always Consult Board

1. **Strategic Direction**
   - New service lines or market entry
   - Major partnerships or alliances
   - Significant pivots or repositioning

2. **Financial**
   - Annual budget and financial plan
   - Large investments (>$50K)
   - Funding decisions (raising capital)

3. **People**
   - Executive hires (C-suite, VP level)
   - Compensation and equity plans
   - Major organizational changes

4. **Risk**
   - Legal/compliance issues
   - Ethical concerns or dilemmas
   - Reputation-threatening situations

### CEO Can Decide (Report to Board)

- Day-to-day operations
- Tactical execution
- Normal course of business
- Hiring below VP level

## Board Member Responsibilities

### All Board Members

**Fiduciary Duties**:
- Duty of care (informed decisions)
- Duty of loyalty (act in company's best interest)
- Duty of good faith (honest and ethical)

**Time Commitment**:
- 4 quarterly meetings (4 hours each)
- 1 annual planning (full day)
- Prep time (4-6 hours per quarter)
- Ad-hoc CEO consultation
- **Total**: ~40-50 hours per year

**Compensation**:
- Equity stake in company
- Per-meeting stipend (optional)
- Reimbursement for expenses

### Board Chair (Dr. Simmons)

**Additional Responsibilities**:
- Set meeting agendas with CEO
- Facilitate board meetings
- CEO coaching and development
- Board effectiveness evaluation
- Crisis management leadership

**Time Commitment**: 2x normal board member (80-100 hours/year)

## Integration with Executive Knowledge System

### Synergy with C-Suite

**Board uses C-Suite knowledge**:
- Reviews executive experience logs for context
- Considers patterns and learnings
- Validates strategic decisions against operational reality

**C-Suite uses Board guidance**:
- Implements strategic direction from board
- Reports outcomes back to board
- Seeks board input on major decisions

### Example Flow

1. **CTO discovers pattern**: API/UI Boundary works well
2. **CTO logs experience**: Documents in executive system
3. **Board reviews**: Sees operational success
4. **Board advises**: "This could be our methodology differentiator"
5. **CMO implements**: Markets "Hybrid AI Implementation Methodology"
6. **Board evaluates**: Measures impact on sales
